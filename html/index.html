About Us

MedConnect is an advanced web application for heathcare providers and patients, that provides mobility and convenience. It incorporates most common and widly used aspects of healthcare information systems into a web friendly user interface. The design and user interface takes in to consideration the influence of smart phones. The idea behind MedConnect is to provide convenience and health tracking tools by the smart and effective usage of web technology. MedConnect is designed, developed and promoted by SafsieCare. MedConnect's online hospital is aimed at providing health care services at your doorstep. With our features, you are able to connect with professional medical practioners in the comfort of your homes. Forget the countless calls to the physician's office trying to get an appointent. With MedConnect's appointments feature, you can book appoinments online at your convenience. With our database, you can access your files. Including Lab reports, doctor's diagnosis and prescriptions. You can also very easily, gain access to prsecribed drugs.

Safsie is designed and developed to help automate, assist and streamline healthcare service delivery

The app was developed in such a way that it can be easily used to conduct the booking of appointment and drug purchase by integrating the traditional healthcare services.
